NEU Game Studio Club Games

Games

The following games were all created in Game Studio Club over a series of game jams. Feel free to click on them to try them out.

Froggy Driver

Play as a car driver who is undergoing constant acceleration and can only guide the car with the steering wheel. Collect the key to unlock your glovebox before the demon can reach you.

Eldritch Escape

You are a worker in a glass factory, who came to the office on an off-day to check up on a strange work request put in from one of your supervisors anonymously...Wander through a maze of dark corridors and find the missing gems you need to unlock the door. And avoid crossing paths with the monster that lurks in the halls!

BIRD2300

You are a student in class who has to participate during class. The problem is, you are very lazy and want to play video games instead. In order to do both, you decide to play BIRD GUN (deluxe). Collect bird facts in order to participate in class and try to get that S grade in participation!

3 Great Games

Play as all your favorite characters in 3 GREAT GAMES! Escape as Cactus Man! Destroy the enemy as Tank! Fight against the alien horde as Laser Ship! Relive your memories! Watch them decay. Watch them decay. Watch them decay...
Watch out for the debug menu! You don't want to break something! Or fix it.

Angel Over Jaywalker

Play as an angel who is protecting a person walking on their phone while aimlessly heading towards danger. 

Roll The Dice

Play as a die that is trying to cross a maze. The catch however is that the die can only move the respective number of spaces on the side you select. So make it to the end, and don't fall off the edge!

Bouncer

Play as a bouncer for a nightclub. Choose who you let in and who you BOUNCE! The reputation of the club and your pay is dependent on this so choose wisely. Will you get the HIGH SCORE?!

Cave Walker

Travel the depths of Earth in this open world 3D RPG adventure game. This is one of our long form projects presented at the CAMD showcase Spring 2022

Spooky Horse Racing

A split-screen multiplayer racing game. Beat your friends in a fun quick and (somewhat difficult) racing experience. [This game was brought to fall fest where players competed live during the event]

Pendulum

Solve puzzles while trying to maintain your sanity in an ever-changing environment.

Secure and Neutralize

Welcome to the Backrooms, Operator 28. Locate the phased in Sectors 4 and 5, and discreetly handle them. Maintain secrecy and be cautious of unknowns. If encountered, stay calm, avoid contact, and sneak away. Good luck.

Bedtime

It's 9 pm, past your bedtime. You hear your stomach growling.You need a snack...However, your parents are having a dinner party downstairs. Use the darkness as cover as you conduct your heist to avoid any awkward conversations!

Rootkit

The year is 2003, and scams are running rampant across the internet. After tricking some poor fool into clicking a suspicious link, you've gained access to their computer's files. Wield the power of 1s and 0s to fight off the Firewall's minions and brick the drive!

Yggdrassil

Delve into the roots of the World Tree to save the myths that wrought it.

Let The Kid Win

Developed for the Hidden Heroes Game Jam. Control your spin strength to land on the desired snakes or ladders. And don't forget to let your kid win!

Interitus

When the last known location of her estranged friend leads Evelyn to a remote castle, she discovers quickly that death and decay are not equal. Escape the force that keeps the residents trapped here through making the tough decisions necessary to survive in this horror visual novel.

Station Obscurum

Amidst the vastness of space, cries for help can be easily unheard, and dangers lurk in every corner. You play as an astronaut in search of parts to repair your ship, and stumble upon an abandoned space station. Explore the decrepit station, brave its terrors, unravel its mysteries, and secure the necessary components to save yourself.

Museum Project

Explore and survive a massive art museum installed on Earth by unknown means. Craft tools to navigate the environment while staying connected to the people you left behind. Catalog the exhibits and, ultimately, decide their fate.

Checking In

A playful criticism of the American healthcare system, Checking In transports you to an ER desk and forces you to make difficult decisions about which patients to help. Try to reach company goals and keep your job at the end of a stressful, fast-paced week! Developed in Sept. 2023 for the Ludum Dare 54 Game Jam.

Claustrophobia

You are an evil psychiatrist. 'your' patients have been institutionalized for extreme claustrophobia. Make them feel small.

Movement Shooter

This is a movement shooter where you power up your gun by bouncing off of walls. More charges equals more bullets and more recoil. Shoot all the red cubes in a level without being crushed or falling off the level. The number in the top right is the count of enemies left.

Seat Fighter

The jerk next to you is hogging the armrest... but he doesn't know that you're the legendary SEAT FIGHTER! Our game supports single-player and two-player couch coop!

Requiem Of Abadon

Use items you find in the a small police and gas station to find the key to escape the area while an invisible enemy, heard through 3D noise wanders through the area.

Backwards-Knees Barry

Imagine a legend. A man. A beast. These things have been juggled into the shuffle of our human experience and the horrific nature of entertainment. An unlikely hero arises against a menacing force. A nation once thought decrepit and useless is given new life. A Story. No. A Legend
